Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: We don't collect your data. Period.

What data does Appstinence access?

Appstinence uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to see which apps you open and how long you use them. This data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted, uploaded, stored on any server, or shared with anyone — including us.

What data do we collect?

None. Appstinence has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking pixels, no third-party SDKs, and no advertising. We literally cannot see your data. We don't know who you are, what apps you use, or how often you use them.

Data storage

All data — including your screen time statistics, intervention history, streak counts, and preferences — is stored locally on your device in encrypted app storage. If you delete Appstinence, all data is permanently deleted. There is no cloud backup of your Appstinence data.

Third-party services

Appstinence contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or tracking of any kind. The only external service interaction is with Apple's StoreKit for processing purchases.

Photos

Appstinence can save a wallpaper image to your Photos library during setup. This is the only interaction with your photo library. We never read or access your existing photos.

Purchases

Appstinence offers a 7-day free trial with full access. After the trial, basic features remain free. Appstinence Pro unlocks all features through a monthly subscription ($4.99/month), annual subscription ($29.99/year), or one-time lifetime purchase ($59.99). All purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. We never see your payment information. Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings at any time.

Children's privacy

Appstinence does not collect data from any user of any age. We comply with COPPA and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian has questions, they can contact us at getappstinence@gmail.com.

Your rights

You can delete all Appstinence data at any time by deleting the app. Since we don't collect or store any data on our servers, there is nothing to request deletion of. You own your data because it never leaves your device.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you through the app before the changes take effect. The latest version will always be available at this page.
